### STATED POSITIONS

**On Hospital Consolidation (repeated, explicit):**
> "Inadequate payments and historic inflation will force independent physician practices to close their doors, leading to increasingly more costly consolidation in the health care market and forcing patients to a more expensive site of service."

> "My younger partners were absorbed by the hospital because they could no longer stay alive financially."

> "Private practice doctors are being forced to close their doors or sell out to large hospitals... even though the care they provide is more personal, higher quality, and cheaper."

**On Medicare Cuts:**
> Medicare physician reimbursements have fallen "an inflation-adjusted 33% since 2001."

> Drew "a line in the sand" against insufficient physician payment updates.

> Called the Inflation Reduction Act "the largest heist of Medicare dollars since the program's inception." (Newsweek op-ed, August 11, 2022)

**On Insurers / Medicare Advantage:**
> "You have put profits above patients and you have put profits above those who care for patients." (directed at insurers at congressional hearing)

**On PBMs:**
> Identified PBMs as "middlemen driving up costs" who "negotiate with manufacturers, develop formularies, and even own pharmacies, further consolidating the American health care system."

**On Physician-Owned Hospitals:**
- Confirmed cosponsor of H.R. 977 (Section 6001 repeal). No dedicated POH press release found, but his entire anti-consolidation, pro-physician-independence framework aligns with repeal.

### DISTRICT DATA

**CON Law:** Yes — North Carolina has one of the more restrictive CON laws in the nation (enacted 1978). Covers new facilities, beds, operating rooms, GI rooms, dialysis stations, cardiac catheterization, open-heart surgery, burn ICU, NICU, transplantation, and more.

**2023 Reforms:** Medicaid expansion legislation (signed March 27, 2023) included CON exemptions for psychiatric/chemical dependency facilities, ambulatory surgical facilities in counties with population >125,000, and MRI scanners in urban counties.

**Active Legal Challenge:** Dr. Jay Singleton (ophthalmologist from New Bern, which is IN NC-03) filed suit arguing NC's CON law is unconstitutional. NC Supreme Court issued unanimous ruling Oct. 2024 sending case back to Superior Court. This challenge originates directly from Murphy's district.

**Hospital Consolidation:**
- **ECU Health** (formerly Vidant Health, rebranded Jan. 2022) dominates the district. A 1,447-bed not-for-profit system serving 1.4 million people across 29 eastern NC counties. Only Level I trauma center east of Raleigh.
- **2023 Clinic Closures:** ECU Health closed 5 regional clinics after losing $46 million in FY2022: Women's Care in Williamston, Family Medicine in Jacksonville, Immediate Care in Wilson, Family Medicine in Aurora, Family Medicine in Snow Hill.
- ECU Health North Hospital (Roanoke Rapids) discontinued inpatient behavioral health services in Feb. 2023.
- District is a textbook example of rural hospital consolidation driving reduced access.
