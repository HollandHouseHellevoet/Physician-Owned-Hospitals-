### STATED POSITIONS

**On Physician Advocacy:**
- Consistently frames healthcare policy from the physician perspective
- Has spoken about administrative burdens on practicing doctors, the Medicare payment cliff, and consolidation harming independent practice

- Publicly and actively supported reform; one of her marquee healthcare issues

**On Independent Practice:**
- As an ophthalmologist who practiced independently, she is sympathetic to independent physician practice models. Ophthalmology is one of the specialties most affected by hospital acquisition.
